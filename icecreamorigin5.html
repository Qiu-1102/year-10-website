Regional characteristics:

Italian Gelato (low fat and low air content), American high-fat ice cream, and Japanese liquid nitrogen technology.

Trivia:

New Zealand has the highest per capita consumption in the world (28.4 liters/year), and Dubai has launched a luxury version containing gold foil (¥6,800/portion).

Key turning points:

From the exclusive domain of royalty to the masses, technology (refrigeration, machinery) and globalized trade (spices, sugar) were the core driving forces.
