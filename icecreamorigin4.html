Industrialization and globalization (19th-20th century)

Technological innovation:

The hand-cranked ice cream machine (Nancy Johnson) in 1843 and the electric ice cream machine in 1920 greatly improved production efficiency.

Contribution of the United States：

The first commercial ice cream factory in 1851 (Jacob Fussell) and the invention of the cone at the 1904 St. Louis World's Fair.

Variety of flavors:

Classic flavors such as vanilla (derived from Mexican orchids) and chocolate are established.
